ASIA SYNDROME

ASIA syndrome (Autoimmune/Inflammatory Syndrome Induced by Adjuvants) is a condition in which the immune system reacts abnormally to foreign substances in the body. In the case of silicone injections (biopolymers) (substances injected for aesthetic purposes), the body can identify them as a foreign agent and generate an inflammatory response that affects different parts of the body.

In medical reviews of ASIA, typical recurring symptoms include chronic fatigue, muscle pain, joint pain, fever, and cognitive difficulty (memory/concentration).
